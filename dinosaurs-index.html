An Introduction to Dinosaurs

Dinosaurs are widely known as the coolest most badass creatures to ever inhabit the small cosmic rock we call Earth. There have been many kinds of Dinosaurs each with its own distinguishing features and charms. There is no wrong favorite Dinosaur.

The purpose of this website is to increase awareness and educate the public about Dinosaurs. There are many myths that have cropped up over the years that spread devious misinformation about our beloved scaley friends. It is my goal to debunk these myths and provide accurate information for all.

DinoFact #1

Dinosaurs share much in common with Humans. For instance, when both Dinosaurs and Humans were polled to find their favorite pie, nearly identical percentages replied 'cake'.
